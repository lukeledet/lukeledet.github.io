Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Rowing Goals, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines our fundamental commitment: we will never distribute, sell, or give away your data to any third parties under any circumstances.

Data Collection and Usage

We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide you with our service:

  • Your Concept2 workout data, which we sync directly from your logbook
  • Basic account information required for authentication
  • Goals and preferences you set within the application

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your workout information in your personal dashboard
  • Tracking progress towards your goals
  • Providing you with insights about your rowing activities

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

Our Promise

We will:

  • Never sell your personal data
  • Never share your information with third parties
  • Never use your data for marketing purposes
  • Never distribute your workout information

Data Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your personal information and workout data will be permanently removed from our systems.
